\textbf{Path:} \texttt{:id} (int) \newline

Partial update. Only specified fields modified. & 
200 OK | 404 Not Found | 400 Bad Request \\

DELETE & \texttt{/:table/:id} & 
\textbf{Path:} \texttt{:id} (int) & 
Deletes record by primary key. & 
200 OK: \texttt{\{ok, message\}} | 404 Not Found \\

POST & \texttt{/query} & 
\textbf{Body:} \texttt{\{"sql": "SELECT ... FROM ..."\}} \newline
\textbf{Restriction:} SELECT-only queries permitted & 
Executes read-only SELECT queries, others blocked for security. & 
200 OK: JSON array | 400 Bad Request | 403 Forbidden \\
